Banana Cue

Banana Cue is a quick and easy dessert made by lightly cooking bananas with sugar until soft and sweet.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas (saba or regular bananas)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil or butter

Instructions

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan.
  2. Add bananas and cook until slightly soft.
  3. Sprinkle sugar and cook until caramelized.
  4. Serve warm.
