Steamed Vegetables

Steamed Vegetables is a healthy and light dish made by cooking vegetables with steam to keep their natural flavor and nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, broccoli, cauliflower, or beans)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water for steaming

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot with a steamer.
  2. Add vegetables to the steamer.
  3. Steam for 5â€“7 minutes until tender.
  4. Season with salt and serve warm.
