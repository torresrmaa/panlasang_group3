Beef Stir-Fry

Beef Stir-Fry is a quick and tasty dish made with sliced beef cooked with vegetables in a savory sauce—perfect for busy days.

Ingredients

  • 500 g beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, or cabbage)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onion.
  2. Add beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce, then mix well.
  4. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3–5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
