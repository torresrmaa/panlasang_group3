Pork Adobo

Pork Adobo is one of the most popular Filipino dishes. It is known for its savory, tangy, and garlicky flavor, simmered slowly until the pork becomes tender and rich.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add pork and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and water. Do not stir.
  4. Add bay leaves and peppercorns.
  5. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for 40–50 minutes.
  6. Simmer until sauce thickens and pork becomes tender.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
