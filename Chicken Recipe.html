Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish made by simmering chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices until tender and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, garlic, vinegar, bay leaves, and peppercorns in a pot.
  2. Add water and bring to a boil.
  3. Lower heat and simmer for 30â€“40 minutes until chicken is tender.
  4. Serve hot with rice.
