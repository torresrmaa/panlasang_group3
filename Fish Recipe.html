Fried Fish

Fried Fish is a quick and crispy dish made by seasoning fish and pan-frying it until golden brown. Itâ€™s simple, tasty, and perfect with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole fish (tilapia or bangus), cleaned
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Season the fish with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Fry the fish until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with rice.
